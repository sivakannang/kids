# Food Words - Theme Based Reading

**Focus**: Food vocabulary and eating-related words
**Goal**: Recognize common foods and eating words

---

## Indian Foods (Common in Your Home)

```
rice   dosa   idly   roti   curry
paste  dal    sambar  rasam  chutney
```

### Read Aloud

- **rice** - grain staple
- **dosa** - thin pancake
- **idly** - steamed cake
- **roti** - wheat bread
- **curry** - spiced dish
- **paste** - ground paste
- **dal** - lentil curry
- **sambar** - vegetable curry
- **rasam** - spicy soup
- **chutney** - ground condiment

### Small Sentences

```
We eat rice every day.
Dosa is crispy.
Idly is soft.
Roti is round.
Mom makes curry.
Add paste to the dish.
Dal is healthy.
Sambar is tasty.
Rasam is hot.
Chutney is spicy.
```

## Fruits

```
apple   mango   banana  orange  grape

```

### Read Aloud

- **apple** - round red fruit
- **mango** - yellow sweet fruit
- **banana** - long yellow fruit
- **orange** - round fruit
- **grape** - small round fruit
- **guava** - sweet green fruit
- **papaya** - orange fruit
- **melon** - sweet fruit
- **lemon** - sour fruit

## Proteins & Staples

```
egg    milk   meat   fish   cheese
bread  cake   sugar  salt   oil
```

### Read Aloud

- **egg** - white and yellow inside
- **milk** - white drink
- **meat** - animal protein
- **fish** - protein from water
- **cheese** - made from milk
- **bread** - made from wheat
- **cake** - sweet bread
- **sugar** - sweet powder
- **salt** - salty powder
- **oil** - cooking liquid

### Small Sentences

```
Egg is protein.
Milk is white.
Meat is healthy.
Fish is tasty.
Cheese is white and hard.
Bread is soft.
Cake is sweet.
Sugar makes it sweet.
Salt makes it salty.
Use oil for cooking.
```

## Eating Actions & Descriptions

```
eat    drink   taste   bite    chew
swallow  spill  hungry  full    yummy
```

### Read Aloud

- **eat** - consume food
- **drink** - consume liquid
- **taste** - check the flavor
- **bite** - take with teeth
- **chew** - crush with teeth
- **swallow** - take down throat
- **spill** - pour accidentally
- **hungry** - wanting to eat
- **full** - stomach is satisfied
- **yummy** - delicious

### Small Sentences

```
I eat rice.
Drink your milk.
Taste the food.
Take a bite.
Chew well.
Don't spill.
I am hungry.
I am full now.
This is yummy.
Let's eat together.
```
