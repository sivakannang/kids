# School Words - Theme Based Reading

**Focus**: Words related to school and classroom
**Goal**: Build confidence with school-related vocabulary

---

## School Places & Things

```
school  class  desk   chair  book   pen    bag
bell    board  room   gate   play
```

### Read Aloud

- **school** - place of learning
- **class** - classroom
- **desk** - table in class
- **chair** - seat in class
- **book** - pages of stories/lessons
- **pen** - for writing
- **bag** - for carrying things
- **bell** - makes sound for time
- **board** - for teacher to write
- **room** - classroom
- **gate** - entrance to school
- **play** - have fun, games

### Small Sentences

```
I go to school.
The class is big.
My desk is clean.
I sit on the chair.
I read a book.
Write with your pen.
Put it in your bag.
The bell rang.
The teacher writes on the board.
We play in the play area.
```

## School People

```
teacher  student  friend  class mate  girl   boy
sister   brother  appa    amma
```

### Read Aloud

- **teacher** - person who teaches
- **student** - person who learns
- **friend** - person you like
- **class mate** - friend in class
- **girl** - female student
- **boy** - male student
- **sister** - sibling
- **brother** - sibling
- **appa** - father (comes to pick you up)
- **amma** - mother (comes to pick you up)

```
My class mate is smart.

```

## School Activities

```
read    write   learn   teach   play
sit     stand   listen  speak   ask
help    copy    draw    sing    dance
```

### Read Aloud

- **read** - look at words and understand
- **write** - make letters with pen
- **learn** - gain knowledge
- **teach** - show how to do something
- **play** - have fun with games
- **sit** - be on chair
- **stand** - be on feet
- **listen** - hear and pay attention
- **speak** - say words
- **ask** - request/question
- **help** - assist someone
- **copy** - write the same as teacher
- **draw** - make pictures
- **sing** - make music with voice
- **dance** - move to music

### Small Sentences

```
I read books in class.
Write the letters.
We learn new things.
The teacher teaches us.
We play at break time.
Sit on your chair.
Stand up when teacher calls.
Listen to the teacher.
Speak clear words.
Ask your question.
Help your friend.
Copy from the board.
Draw a picture.
We sing a song.
We dance together.
```

## School Schedule Words

```
morning  break   lunch   time    bell
early    late    come    go      home
```

### Read Aloud

- **morning** - early part of day
- **break** - short rest time
- **lunch** - midday meal
- **time** - hours and minutes
- **bell** - sounds for schedule
- **early** - before the set time
- **late** - after the set time
- **come** - arrive
- **go** - leave
- **home** - your house

### Small Sentences

```
School starts in the morning.
We have break time.
We eat lunch in school.
It is time for math.
The bell rang.
Come early to school.
Don't come late.
Go outside to play.
I go home after school.
```

## Reading Practice - School Theme

### Read Aloud (Slow)

**Part 1: School Day**

```
I go to school in the morning.
My bag has books and pen.
I sit at my desk.
The teacher is kind.
My friend sits next to me.
```

**Part 2: In Class**

```
We read books.
We write letters.
We copy from the board.
We listen to the teacher.
We stand up when asked.
```

**Part 3: Break & Play**

```
The bell rang for break.
We go out to play.
We play games.
We sing and dance.
We come back to class.
```

### Read Aloud (Faster)

```
I go to school every morning.
My teacher teaches us to read and write.
I read books and write letters.
I play with my friends at break.
We eat lunch together.
The bell rings at home time.
Appa comes to pick me up.
```
