# Action Words - Theme Based Reading

**Focus**: Verbs and action words for daily activities
**Goal**: Build vocabulary for things we do

---

## Body Movement Actions

```
run    jump   walk   skip   hop
sit    stand  lie    crawl  climb
```

### Read Aloud

- **run** - move fast on feet
- **jump** - leap off ground
- **walk** - move slowly on feet
- **skip** - hop with rhythm
- **hop** - bounce on one foot
- **sit** - be on chair/floor
- **stand** - be on feet
- **lie** - be on ground
- **crawl** - move on hands and knees
- **climb** - go up higher

### Small Sentences

```
I run very fast.
Jump high!
We walk to school.
Skip with your friend.
Hop on one foot.
Sit on the chair.
Stand up, please.
Lie on the bed.
The baby crawls.
Climb the tree.
```

## Hand & Object Actions

```
write   draw    hold   catch   throw
pick    drop    push   pull    open
close   cut     tear   fold    mix
```

### Read Aloud

- **write** - make letters with pen
- **draw** - make pictures
- **hold** - keep in hand
- **catch** - grab something coming
- **throw** - toss something
- **pick** - grab something
- **drop** - let fall
- **push** - move away
- **pull** - move toward
- **open** - unfold/unlock
- **close** - fold/lock
- **cut** - slice with knife
- **tear** - rip with hands
- **fold** - bend paper
- **mix** - combine together

### Small Sentences

```
Write your name.
Draw a picture.
Hold my hand.
Catch the ball.
Throw the ball.
Pick up your toys.
Don't drop the cup.
Push the door.
Pull the rope.
Open the window.
Close the door.
Cut the bread.
Tear the paper.
Fold your dress.
Mix the colors.
```

## Face & Sound Actions

```
sing    speak   say    listen   laugh
smile   cry     eat    drink    taste
```

### Read Aloud

- **sing** - make music with voice
- **speak** - say words
- **say** - utter words
- **listen** - hear and pay attention
- **laugh** - make happy sound
- **smile** - show happy face
- **cry** - make sad sound with tears
- **eat** - consume food
- **drink** - consume liquid
- **taste** - check flavor

### Small Sentences

```
Sing a song.
Speak carefully.
Say the word.
Listen to me.
Don't cry.
Smile, please.
This is funny, I laugh.
Eat your rice.
Drink your milk.
Taste the food.
```

## Mental & Daily Actions

```
think   know   learn   try    help
rest    sleep  wake    cook   play
work    read   teach   make   come
```

### Read Aloud

- **think** - use your mind
- **know** - have knowledge
- **learn** - gain knowledge
- **try** - attempt to do
- **help** - assist someone
- **rest** - relax
- **sleep** - be unconscious at night
- **wake** - get up
- **cook** - prepare food
- **play** - have fun
- **work** - do activity
- **read** - look at words and understand
- **teach** - show how to do
- **make** - create something
- **come** - arrive

### Small Sentences

```
Think about this.
I know this word.
I learn new things.
Try again.
Help your friend.
Rest after school.
Sleep at night.
Wake up in the morning.
Mom will cook.
Let's play now.
Dad works every day.
I read books.
Teacher teaches us.
Make a picture.
Come here.
```

## Reading Practice - Action Theme

### Read Aloud (Slow)

**Part 1: Morning Actions**

```
I wake up in the morning.
I stand up.
I drink milk.
I eat my breakfast.
I get ready for school.
```

**Part 2: School Actions**

```
I walk to school.
I sit at my desk.
I write letters.
I draw pictures.
I read books.
```

**Part 3: Play Actions**

```
My friend and I run and jump.
We play games.
We throw and catch the ball.
We laugh and smile.
We have fun together.
```

### Read Aloud (Faster)

```
I run and jump.
I walk and skip.
I sit and write.
I draw and read.
I sing and laugh.
We play and smile.
I eat and drink.
I sleep at night.
I wake in the morning.
Let's have fun!
```
