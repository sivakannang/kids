# People Words - Theme Based Reading

**Focus**: Words related to people and their relationships
**Goal**: Recognize people-related vocabulary

---

## Family Members

```
mom   dad   brother  sister
grandpa  grandma  uncle   aunt
cousin  baby  son  daughter
```

### Read Aloud

- **mom** - mother
- **dad** - father
- **brother** - male sibling
- **sister** - female sibling
- **grandpa** - father's father
- **grandma** - father's mother
- **uncle** - parent's brother
- **aunt** - parent's sister
- **cousin** - uncle/aunt's child
- **baby** - small child
- **son** - male child
- **daughter** - female child

### Small Sentences

```
My mom is kind.
My dad is strong.
I have a brother.
I have a sister.
Grandpa is old.
Grandma makes food.
Uncle is funny.
Aunt is nice.
Cousin is my friend.
The baby is small.
I am my parent's son/daughter.
```

## People Descriptions

```
boy     girl    man     woman
child   baby    people  person
young   old     big     small
```

### Read Aloud

- **boy** - male child
- **girl** - female child
- **man** - adult male
- **woman** - adult female
- **child** - young person
- **baby** - newborn/very young
- **people** - many persons
- **person** - one individual
- **young** - not old
- **old** - not young
- **big** - large size
- **small** - little size

### Small Sentences

```
I am a boy/girl.
The man is tall.
The woman is here.
The child is playing.
The baby is sleeping.
Many people are here.
Every person is different.
Young people run fast.
Old people walk slowly.
Big person helps small person.
```

## People Qualities

```
honest   kind    gentle   strong
brave    smart   clever   helpful
lazy     rude    mean     foolish
```

### Read Aloud

- **honest** - telling truth
- **kind** - nice and gentle
- **gentle** - soft and careful
- **strong** - powerful
- **brave** - fearless
- **smart** - intelligent
- **clever** - quick-thinking
- **helpful** - gives help
- **lazy** - doesn't work hard
- **rude** - not polite
- **mean** - unkind
- **foolish** - not wise

### Small Sentences


## Relationship Words

```
friend   best friend  love   care
help    trusted person  like  respect
family   group    team   together
```

### Read Aloud

- **friend** - person we like
- **best friend** - closest friend
- **love** - deep affection
- **care** - think about someone
- **help** - assist someone
- **trusted person** - person we believe in
- **like** - enjoy someone
- **respect** - honor someone
- **family** - relatives
- **group** - many people together
- **team** - people working together
- **together** - with others

### Small Sentences

```
I have a friend.
She is my best friend.
I love my family.
I care for my friend.
Help your friends.
I trust my teacher.
I like my classmate.
Respect your parents.
Family is important.
We are a group.
We are a team.
We play together.
```

## Reading Practice - People Theme

### Read Aloud (Slow)

**Part 1: Family**

```
My family is important.
My mom is kind.
My dad is strong.
I have a brother.
I have a sister.
```

**Part 2: People Around Us**

```
My teacher teaches us.
My friend plays with me.
The doctor helps sick people.
The nurse is gentle.
The police keeps us safe.
```

**Part 3: Good People**

```
Be kind to everyone.
Be honest always.
Be brave.
Help your friend.
Respect your parents.
Love your family.
```

### Read Aloud (Faster)

```
My family has mom, dad, brother, and sister.
My friend is my best friend.
Teachers teach us.
Doctors help us.
Police keeps us safe.
Be kind, honest, and brave.
Help others.
Respect everyone.
Love your family.
Together we are strong.
```
