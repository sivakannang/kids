# Level 0: One-Letter Words

**Focus**: Letter and single-word recognition
**Time**: 10 minutes daily
**Goal**: Instant recognition of letters as words

---

## Vowels - Read Aloud

These letters are also words:

```
a    I
```

### Practice

Point at each letter and read it aloud. Say it 3-4 times.

```
a   a   a   a
I   I   I   I
```

### Read with Pictures

**a** - a cat, a dog, a book, a ball, a tree
**I** - I run, I play, I read, I sing, I dance

---

## Simple Consonant Words

In English, only **I** and **a** are one-letter words by themselves.

But here are some one-letter sounds we use:

```
b (for ball)      d (for dog)
c (for cat)       f (for fish)
m (for mom)       n (for no)
```

---

## Copy Writing Practice

**Copy these letters 5 times:**

```
a _ _ _ _ _

I _ _ _ _ _
```

**Read and copy:**

```
a dog  _ _ _ _ _

I am   _ _ _ _ _
```

## Revision - Mixed Words

Point and read fast:

```
a   I   a   I   a
I   a   I   a   I
```

**Can you read these without stopping?** ✓
