# Level 2: Daily Life Sentences

**Focus**: Reading sentences about everyday activities
**Time**: 10-15 minutes daily
**Goal**: Build confidence with real-life sentences

---

## Morning Routine

```
I wake up in the morning.
I get out of bed.
I drink a glass of water.
I wash my face.
I eat breakfast.
I sit on the chair.
I eat my food.
```

### Read Aloud
Read each sentence slowly, then faster.

### Copy Practice
```
I wake up in the morning.    _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
I eat my breakfast.          _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
```

I wear my shoes.

I go to my class.

## In the Classroom

```
My teacher is here.
We sit in our class.
The teacher reads out words.
I read the words.
I write in my book.
I copy from the board.
I do my work.
The bell rings.
We go to play.
```

## Play Time

```

We have fun together.
The bell rings again.
We come back to class.
We are happy and tired.
```

I run to meet him.
