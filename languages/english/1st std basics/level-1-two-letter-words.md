# Level 1: Two-Letter Words

**Focus**: Recognizing common two-letter words instantly
**Time**: 10-15 minutes daily
**Goal**: Read without hesitation

---

## Word Group 1: "is" and "am"

```
is
am
```

### Read Aloud
Say each word 3-4 times:
- "**is**" - used for "he is", "she is", "it is"
- "**am**" - used for "I am"

### Small Sentences
```
I am happy.
It is hot.
She is here.
```

### Copy Practice
```
is  _ _ _ _
am  _ _ _ _

I am happy.    _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
```

## Word Group 2: "we", "he", "me", "be"

```
we
he
me
be
```

### Read Aloud
- **we** - us, together
- **he** - a boy, a man
- **me** - myself
- **be** - (to exist)

### Small Sentences
```
We play together.
He can run.
Call me. (or: Tell me.)
I can be happy.
```

### Copy Practice
```
we  _ _ _ _
he  _ _ _ _
me  _ _ _ _
be  _ _ _ _

We are happy.   _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
```

## Word Group 3: "in", "at", "to", "on"

```
in
at
to
on
```

### Read Aloud
- **in** - inside
- **at** - location/place
- **to** - direction
- **on** - top of something

### Small Sentences
```
I am in school.
We sit at the table.
I go to home.
The book is on the bed.
```

### Copy Practice
```
in  _ _ _ _
at  _ _ _ _
to  _ _ _ _
on  _ _ _ _

Sit at the desk.    _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
```

## Word Group 4: "it", "up", "no", "go"

```
it
up
no
go
```

### Read Aloud
- **it** - the thing
- **up** - direction up
- **no** - negative
- **go** - move/walk

### Small Sentences
```
It is a cat.
Jump up high!
No, I cannot do it.
Go to school.
```

### Copy Practice
```
it  _ _ _ _
up  _ _ _ _
no  _ _ _ _
go  _ _ _ _

Go up the stairs.   _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
```

## Word Group 5: "or", "as", "do", "if"

```
or
as
do
if
```

### Read Aloud
- **or** - choice
- **as** - like/similar
- **do** - action/perform
- **if** - condition

### Small Sentences
```
Would you like milk or juice?
Fast as a cheetah!
I do my homework.
If you are happy, clap.
```

### Copy Practice
```
or  _ _ _ _
as  _ _ _ _
do  _ _ _ _
if  _ _ _ _

Do your homework.    _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
```
