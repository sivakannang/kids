# Reading Practice - Mixed Levels

**Focus**: Mixed reading across different levels
**Time**: 15-20 minutes daily
**Goal**: Build fluency and confidence with variety

---

## Section 1: Word Recognition (5 mins)

### Level 1 Words
Point and read fast (no pausing):

```
I am we he me be in at to on it up no go or as do if
```

### Level 2 Words
Point and read fast:

```
mom dad boy girl son bed cat dog pen bag run sit see can
```

### Level 3 Words
Point and read fast:

```
home that have just very book door time need like rice food good
```

### Level 4 Words
Point and read fast:

```
play help read walk work glad kind calm warm fast blue long here many some
```

## Section 4: Daily Story (5-10 mins)

**Morning to Night Story**

Read this entire story without stopping:

```
I wake up in the morning. It is time to go to school. I get ready.
I eat my breakfast. It is rice and egg. I drink milk. Appa takes me to school.

I sit in the class. My teacher is kind. We read books. I write letters.
I copy from the board. The bell rings. I go to play. My friend and I play games.
We jump and run. We have fun. The bell rings again. We come back to class.

After school, Amma picks me up. We go home. I eat a snack. I rest a little.
Brother and I play. Sister reads a book. Dad comes home. We are happy to see him.

We eat dinner together. The food is good. I eat rice and curry. I drink milk.
We talk and laugh. After dinner, Mom reads a story. I am sleepy. I go to sleep.
Good night!
```

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Read Aloud**: Always read aloud to build confidence
2. **Don't Rush**: Take your time, accuracy is more important than speed
3. **Repeat Often**: The same sentences appear multiple times
4. **Connect**: Think about what the sentences mean
5. **Celebrate**: Every correct read is a victory!
