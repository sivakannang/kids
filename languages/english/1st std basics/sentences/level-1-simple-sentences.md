# Level 1: Simple Sentences

**Focus**: Reading very simple 2-3 word sentences
**Time**: 5-10 minutes daily
**Goal**: Start building sentence confidence

---

## Pattern: "I am ___"

```
I am happy.
I am sad.
I am here.
I am big.
I am small.
I am here.
I am ready.
I am okay.
```

### Read Aloud
Say each sentence clearly. Pause between words the first time, then read smoothly.

### Copy Practice
```
I am happy.    _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
```

---

## Pattern: "It is ___"

```
It is hot.
It is cold.
It is red.
It is big.
It is new.
It is good.
It is fun.
It is time.
```

### Read Aloud
Point at each sentence and read without stopping.

### Copy Practice
```
It is hot.    _ _ _ _ _
```

---

## Pattern: "The ___ is ___"

```
The cat is black.
The dog is big.
The sun is hot.
The milk is cold.
The door is open.
The book is new.
The ball is red.
The boy is fast.
```

### Read Aloud
Read each sentence 2-3 times without stopping.

---

## Pattern: "I ___ ___"

```
I can run.
I can jump.
I can read.
I can play.
I can sit.
I can help.
I can see.
I like milk.
```

### Read Aloud

---

## Daily Practice

Day 1-2: Read all sentences 3 times each
Day 3-4: Read and copy 5 sentences
Day 5-6: Read all sentences without stopping
Day 7: Mix and read in any order

---

## Copy Writing Practice

Pick 3 sentences each day and copy:

```
I am happy.       _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
I can run.        _ _ _ _ _ _
The cat is black. _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
```

## Revision - Speed Reading

```

I can play.        It is good.

```
