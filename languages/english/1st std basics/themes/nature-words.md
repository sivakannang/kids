# Nature Words - Theme Based Reading

**Focus**: Nature and outdoor environment vocabulary
**Goal**: Recognize natural world words

## Sky & Weather

```
sun    moon   star   sky    cloud
rain   wind   snow   thunder storm
```

### Read Aloud

- **sun** - bright, hot, gives light
- **moon** - bright at night
- **star** - tiny light at night
- **sky** - above us
- **cloud** - white fluffy in sky
- **rain** - water from sky
- **wind** - moving air
- **snow** - cold white rain
- **thunder** - loud sound in rain
- **storm** - very bad weather

### Small Sentences

```
The sun is hot and bright.
The moon shines at night.
Stars are far away.
The sky is blue.
Clouds are white.
It is raining today.
The wind is cold.
Snow is white and cold.
Listen to the thunder.
The storm is strong.
```

## Plants & Trees

```
tree   plant  flower  leaf   grass
bush   seed   root    branch flower
```

### Read Aloud

- **tree** - big plant with trunk
- **plant** - growing thing
- **flower** - pretty part of plant
- **leaf** - green part on branch
- **grass** - green on ground
- **bush** - small tree
- **seed** - from plant
- **root** - under ground
- **branch** - from tree trunk
- **flower** - colorful plant

### Small Sentences

```
The tree is big.
Plants need water.
The flower is red.
Leaves are green.
Grass is soft.
Bushes are small.
Seeds grow into plants.
Roots are under ground.
Branches hold leaves.
Flowers are pretty.
```

## Animals & Insects

```
dog    cat    bird   fish   cow
horse  lion   tiger  snake  butterfly
ant    bee    insect bug    worm
```

### Read Aloud

- **dog** - pet animal
- **cat** - pet animal
- **bird** - has wings, can fly
- **fish** - lives in water
- **cow** - gives milk
- **horse** - big animal
- **lion** - big wild cat
- **tiger** - striped wild cat
- **snake** - long animal
- **butterfly** - pretty insect
- **ant** - tiny insect
- **bee** - makes honey
- **insect** - small animal
- **bug** - small creeping animal
- **worm** - long thin animal

### Small Sentences

```
Dog is my friend.
Cat is soft.
Bird can fly.
Fish lives in water.
Cow gives milk.
Horse is big.
Lion is strong.
Tiger is fast.
Snake is long.
Butterfly is pretty.
Ant is tiny.
Bee makes honey.
Insect is small.
Bug crawls.
Worm is long.
```

## Water & Places

```
water   river   lake   sea    ocean
beach   mountain hill   valley cave
```

### Read Aloud

- **water** - clear liquid
- **river** - flowing water
- **lake** - still water
- **sea** - big water
- **ocean** - very big water
- **beach** - sand by water
- **mountain** - very high place
- **hill** - high place
- **valley** - low place
- **cave** - hole in mountain

### Small Sentences

```
Water is clear.
River flows.
Lake is still.
Sea is big.
Ocean is very big.
Beach has sand.
Mountain is very high.
Hill is high.
Valley is low.
Cave is dark.
```

## Nature Descriptions

```
green   bright  dark   wet   dry
hot    cold   warm   soft   hard
```

### Read Aloud

- **green** - color of plants
- **bright** - full of light
- **dark** - no light
- **wet** - with water
- **dry** - no water
- **hot** - high temperature
- **cold** - low temperature
- **warm** - comfortably hot
- **soft** - not hard
- **hard** - not soft

### Small Sentences

```
Grass is green.
The sun is bright.
The night is dark.
Rain makes it wet.
After rain, it is dry.
The sun is hot.
Winter is cold.
Spring is warm.
Flower is soft.
Rock is hard.
```
