# Family Words - Theme Based Reading

**Focus**: Words related to family and family members
**Real Names**: Using your real family names
**Goal**: Recognize family-related vocabulary instantly

---

## Family Members (Your Family)

```
Siva      Kavi        Kavikuyil
Prabanjan Gayathri
```

### Read Aloud

Say each name clearly:
- **Siva** - (First name)
- **Kavi** - (Name)
- **Kavikuyil** - (Name)
- **Prabanjan** - (Name)
- **Gayathri** - (Name)

### Family Roles

```
appa   amma   brother   sister   me   you
```

### Read Aloud
- **appa** - father (Tamil/Telugu)
- **amma** - mother (Tamil/Telugu)
- **brother** - male sibling
- **sister** - female sibling
- **me** - myself
- **you** - person I'm talking to

### Small Sentences Using Family Names

```
Siva is appa.
Gayathri is amma.
Kavi is brother.
Kavikuyil is sister.
I am me. (I am Kavi / I am Kavikuyil)
You are smart.
```

## Family-Related Feelings & Actions

```
love   hug   help   play   care
happy  safe  home
```

### Read Aloud
- **love** - deep feeling of affection
- **hug** - embrace someone
- **help** - assist family
- **play** - have fun together
- **care** - think about someone
- **happy** - joyful
- **safe** - protected
- **home** - family place

### Small Sentences

```
I love my family.
Appa gives me a hug.
Amma helps me.
We play together at home.
I care for my sister.
My family makes me happy.
I feel safe with appa and amma.
Home is where my family is.
```

## Family Activities

```
eat    cook   rest   sleep   wake
call   visit  hug    kiss    talk
```

### Read Aloud
- **eat** - consume food
- **cook** - prepare food
- **rest** - relax
- **sleep** - at night
- **wake** - get up
- **call** - phone/shout to someone
- **visit** - go see someone
- **hug** - embrace
- **kiss** - touch with lips
- **talk** - speak/chat

### Small Sentences

```
We eat rice together.
Amma cooks good food.
I rest after school.
I sleep in my bed.
I wake up in the morning.
Call appa on the phone.
We visit grandma on Sunday.
Give amma a hug.
Kiss appa good night.
I talk to my brother.
```

## Possessive Words

```
my    your   his    her    our
```

### Read Aloud
- **my** - belongs to me
- **your** - belongs to you
- **his** - belongs to a boy/man
- **her** - belongs to a girl/woman
- **our** - belongs to us all

### Small Sentences

```
This is my book.
Your bag is red.
His pen is blue.
Her home is big.
Our family is happy.
My amma is kind.
Your appa is strong.
```

## Reading Practice - Family Theme

### Read Aloud (Slow)

**Part 1:**
```
Siva is appa.
Gayathri is amma.
Kavi is my brother.
Kavikuyil is my sister.
I love my family.
```

**Part 2:**
```
My appa helps me.
My amma cooks rice.
My brother plays with me.
My sister reads with me.
We have a happy home.
```

**Part 3:**
```
I sleep in my bed.
I wake in the morning.
We eat together.
We talk and laugh.
We are safe and happy.
```

### Read Aloud (Faster)

Try reading these without stopping:
```
My appa is strong.
My amma is kind.
My brother is fast.
My sister is smart.
My family loves me.
I love my family.
We are all very happy.
Home is the best place.
```
