# Feelings Words - Theme Based Reading

**Focus**: Emotions and feelings vocabulary
**Goal**: Recognize and describe feelings instantly

---

## Happy Feelings

```
happy   glad    joy     smile   laugh
funny   merry   cheerful  excited
```

### Read Aloud

- **happy** - feeling of joy
- **glad** - pleased and contented
- **joy** - great happiness
- **smile** - happy face expression
- **laugh** - make happy sound
- **funny** - amusing/makes us laugh
- **merry** - jovial and happy
- **cheerful** - bright and happy
- **excited** - very enthusiastic

### Small Sentences

```
I am happy.
I am glad to see you.
Toys bring me joy.
Smile at your friend.
We laugh together.
The joke is funny.
Merry Christmas!
She is cheerful.
I am excited about school.
```

---

## Sad Feelings

```
sad    angry   upset   cry    sorry
tired  boring  lonely  scared  worried
```

### Read Aloud

- **sad** - feeling unhappy
- **angry** - feeling upset
- **upset** - disturbed/angry
- **cry** - shed tears from sadness
- **sorry** - feeling regret
- **tired** - lacking energy
- **boring** - not interesting
- **lonely** - feeling alone
- **scared** - feeling fear
- **worried** - feeling concerned

### Small Sentences

```
Don't be sad.
He is angry.
I am upset.
Don't cry.
I am sorry.
I am tired.
This is boring.
She feels lonely.
Don't be scared.
I am worried about the test.
```

## Calm & Peaceful Feelings

```
calm    quiet   peaceful  gentle   soft
relax   rest    beautiful  kind    loved
```

### Read Aloud

- **calm** - peaceful and quiet
- **quiet** - making no noise
- **peaceful** - without stress
- **gentle** - soft and kind
- **soft** - not harsh
- **relax** - become loose and calm
- **rest** - take a break
- **beautiful** - very pleasing
- **kind** - nice/gentle
- **loved** - feeling affection

### Small Sentences

```
Be calm.
Quiet, please.
The lake is peaceful.
She is gentle.
Soft music is nice.
Relax after school.
Rest on the bed.
The sunset is beautiful.
Mom is kind to me.
I feel loved.
```

## Descriptive Feelings

```
brave   strong  weak    sick   okay
good    bad     fine    sick   healthy
proud   shame   confident uncertain
```

### Read Aloud

- **brave** - fearless/courageous
- **strong** - powerful
- **weak** - not strong
- **sick** - not healthy
- **okay** - acceptable/fine
- **good** - positive
- **bad** - negative
- **fine** - feeling well
- **healthy** - in good health
- **proud** - feeling proud
- **shame** - feeling ashamed
- **confident** - feeling sure
- **uncertain** - not sure

### Small Sentences

```
Be brave.
I am strong.
I feel weak.
I am sick today.
Everything is okay.
This is good.
This is bad.
I am fine.
Eat to be healthy.
I am proud of you.
Don't feel shame.
Be confident.
I am uncertain.
```

## Reading Practice - Feelings Theme

### Read Aloud (Slow)

**Part 1: Happy**

```
I am happy.
I am glad to see you.
I laugh when something is funny.
I smile at my friends.
I am excited to play.
```

**Part 2: Sad**

```
Don't be sad.
Don't be angry.
Don't cry.
Don't be scared.
Don't be worried.
```

**Part 3: Calm & Good**

```
Be calm and quiet.
Be peaceful.
Be gentle and kind.
Rest when tired.
I feel fine.
```

### Read Aloud (Faster)

```
I am happy, glad, and excited.
Don't be sad, angry, or scared.
Be calm, quiet, and peaceful.
Be brave and strong.
Be kind and gentle.
I feel loved and proud.
Everything is okay.
Let's be happy together!
```
