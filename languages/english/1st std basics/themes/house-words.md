# House Words - Theme Based Reading

**Focus**: Words related to home and house items
**Goal**: Recognize house vocabulary for daily use

---

## House Rooms & Areas

```
hall   room   bed    bath   kitchen   door   gate
```

### Read Aloud

- **hall** - big common area
- **room** - sleeping/private space
- **bed** - where we sleep
- **bath** - where we wash
- **kitchen** - where we cook
- **door** - entrance to room
- **gate** - entrance to house

### Small Sentences

```
I am in the hall.
My room is big.
The bed is soft.
I take a bath.
Amma is in the kitchen.
Close the door.
The gate is closed.
```

---

## Furniture & Household Items

```
sofa   chair   table   lamp   fan
wall   floor   roof    window
```

### Read Aloud

- **sofa** - big seat for many people
- **chair** - seat with legs
- **table** - flat surface
- **lamp** - light
- **fan** - keeps air cool
- **wall** - side of room
- **floor** - ground of room
- **roof** - top of house
- **window** - opening for light

### Small Sentences

```
Sit on the sofa.
The chair is hard.
Put it on the table.
The lamp is bright.
The fan is fast.
The wall is white.
The floor is clean.
The roof is red.
Look out the window.
```

## House Descriptions

```
big   small   clean   dirty   new   old
bright  dark  hot    cold   wet   dry
```

### Read Aloud

- **big** - large in size
- **small** - little in size
- **clean** - no dirt
- **dirty** - with dirt
- **new** - recently made
- **old** - not new
- **bright** - full of light
- **dark** - no light
- **hot** - high temperature
- **cold** - low temperature
- **wet** - with water
- **dry** - no water

### Small Sentences

```
Our house is big.
My room is small.
Keep the floor clean.
Don't make it dirty.
We have new furniture.
The house is old.
The lamp makes it bright.
It is dark at night.
The kitchen is hot.
The fan makes it cold.
The floor is wet after bath.
The clothes are dry.
```

## House Activities

```
clean   cook   sleep   wake   sit   stand
open   close   bring   keep   make   take
```

### Read Aloud

- **clean** - make tidy
- **cook** - prepare food
- **sleep** - rest at night
- **wake** - get up
- **sit** - be on a seat
- **stand** - be on feet
- **open** - unlock/unfold
- **close** - lock/fold
- **bring** - carry here
- **keep** - store/place
- **make** - create
- **take** - carry away

### Small Sentences

```
Clean your room.
Mom will cook.
I sleep at night.
I wake up early.
Sit on the chair.
Stand up, please.
Open the door.
Close the window.
Bring water.
Keep your books.
Make your bed.
Take your bag.
```

## Reading Practice - House Theme

### Read Aloud (Slow)

**Part 1: House Rooms**

```
I am in the hall.
The room is small.
My bed is soft.
I take a bath in the bath.
Amma cooks in the kitchen.
```

**Part 2: Furniture**

```
Sit on the sofa.
The chair is hard.
Put it on the table.
The lamp is bright.
The fan is cold.
```

**Part 3: House Activities**

```
I clean my room every week.
My mom cooks every day.
I sleep at night.
I wake in the morning.
We keep our house clean.
```

### Read Aloud (Faster)

```
My house is big and bright.
My room is small and clean.
The table is hard.
The sofa is soft.
The lamp makes it bright.
The fan is fast.
The window is open.
The door is closed.
The floor is dry.
The wall is white.
```
