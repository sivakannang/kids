# Kitchen Words - Theme Based Reading

**Focus**: Words related to cooking and kitchen items
**Goal**: Recognize kitchen vocabulary from daily cooking

---

## Kitchen Utensils & Items

```
cup    plate   spoon   fork   knife   pot    pan
glass  bowl    tray    jug    box     can
```

### Read Aloud

- **cup** - for drinking
- **plate** - for eating
- **spoon** - for scooping
- **fork** - for picking food
- **knife** - for cutting
- **pot** - for cooking
- **pan** - for frying
- **glass** - for cold drinks
- **bowl** - for mixing/serving
- **tray** - for carrying
- **jug** - for water/milk
- **box** - for storage
- **can** - metal container

### Small Sentences

```
Put rice on a plate.
Use a spoon to eat.
The cup is full.
The glass is cold.
Pour water in the bowl.
Cook rice in a pot.
Fry eggs in a pan.
Keep salt in a box.
Drink milk from a glass.
```

## Kitchen Foods (Short Words)

```
rice   bread  milk   egg    salt
oil    sugar  water  tea    meat
```

### Read Aloud

- **rice** - grain staple
- **bread** - baked food
- **milk** - white drink
- **egg** - protein food
- **salt** - seasoning
- **oil** - cooking liquid
- **sugar** - sweet
- **water** - clear drink
- **tea** - hot drink
- **meat** - animal protein

### Small Sentences

```
We eat rice every day.
Bread is soft.
Milk is white.
Eggs are good food.
Add salt to rice.
Use oil for cooking.
Sugar is sweet.
Drink water.
Mom makes tea.
```

## Cooking Actions

```
cook   cut    mix    fry    boil
heat   salt   stir   taste  serve
```

### Read Aloud

- **cook** - prepare food
- **cut** - slice with knife
- **mix** - combine together
- **fry** - cook in oil
- **boil** - cook in hot water
- **heat** - make hot
- **salt** - add salt
- **stir** - mix with spoon
- **taste** - check flavor
- **serve** - give food

### Small Sentences

```
Mom will cook rice.
Cut the bread.
Mix the milk.
Fry the eggs.
Boil water for tea.
Heat the pan.
Add salt here.
Stir the pot.
Taste the food.
Serve the rice.
```

## Describing Kitchen Items & Food

```
hot    cold   sweet   salty   soft
hard   fresh  clean   dirty   good
```

### Read Aloud

- **hot** - high temperature
- **cold** - low temperature
- **sweet** - tastes sweet
- **salty** - has salt
- **soft** - not hard
- **hard** - not soft
- **fresh** - newly made
- **clean** - no dirt
- **dirty** - with dirt
- **good** - tasty

### Small Sentences

```
The tea is hot.
The water is cold.
Cake is sweet.
Salt makes it salty.
Bread is soft.
The nut is hard.
Fresh milk is best.
Keep it clean.
Don't make it dirty.
This food is good.
```

## Reading Practice - Kitchen Theme

```

```

**Part 2: Cooking**

```

Boil water for tea.
Fry the eggs.
Stir the pot.
```

**Part 3: Kitchen Food**

```

```

