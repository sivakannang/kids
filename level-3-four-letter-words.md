# Level 3: Four-Letter Words

**Focus**: Building vocabulary for more complex sentences
**Time**: 20-25 minutes daily
**Goal**: Reading sentences with confidence

---

## Family & Relationships

```
home  that  have  just  very
```

### Read Aloud
- **home** - where we live
- **that** - pointing word
- **have** - possess
- **just** - only / recently
- **very** - extremely

### Small Sentences
```
I go home after school.
That is my dog.
I have a book.
He just came.
This is very good.
```

### Copy Practice
```
home  _ _ _ _ _
have  _ _ _ _ _
that  _ _ _ _ _

I have a home.    _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
```
