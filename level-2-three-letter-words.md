# Level 2: Three-Letter Words

**Focus**: Common three-letter words from daily life
**Time**: 15-20 minutes daily
**Goal**: Instant recognition and sentence building

---

## Family & People Words

```
mom   dad   boy   girl   son
```

### Read Aloud
- **mom** - mother, amma
- **dad** - father, appa
- **boy** - a male child
- **girl** - a female child
- **son** - a boy in a family

### Small Sentences
```
Mom is here.
Dad can help.
The boy runs fast.
The girl is happy.
My son is in school.
```

### Copy Practice
```
mom  _ _ _ _
dad  _ _ _ _
boy  _ _ _ _

Mom is in the kitchen.    _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
```

## Action Words (Verbs)

```

```

### Read Aloud
- **run** - move fast
- **sit** - be on chair

- **see** - look with eyes

### Small Sentences
```

```
